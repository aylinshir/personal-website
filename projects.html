Project 1: Logic Gates Lab

SITE 1101 – Principles of Information Systems

This project was our first team-based lab work for SITE 1101. Together with my teammates, we constructed and tested logic gates (NOT, AND, OR, XOR, NAND) using transistors, resistors, breadboards, and LEDs.

We learned how hardware components work at a fundamental level, followed electrical safety rules, and collaborated effectively under time constraints in the Net Lab. The project strengthened my teamwork, problem-solving, and basic electronics skills.
